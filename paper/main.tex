\paragraph{HAR Model.}
The Heterogeneous Autoregressive model of \citet{corsi2009} is specified as:
\begin{equation}
  RV_{t+h} = \beta_0 + \beta_1 RV_t + \beta_2 RV_{t-5:t} + \beta_3 RV_{t-22:t} + \varepsilon_{t+h},
  \label{eq:har}
\end{equation}
where $RV_{t-k:t}$ denotes the average realized volatility over the previous $k$ days.
